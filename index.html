About

What I look for

If you want me to work with you, there's three things I like for in every opportunity.

Smart People

I maximize for learning on a regular basis, and I think the best way to do that is to surround myself with really smart people. Am I working with people who have superpowers?

Autonomy

I work best when empowered. That doesn't mean I want free reign, but that I feel I have the ability to control my own work and growth. I like to work outside the box.

Impact

I care about my individual contributions impacting the world at large. How much will what I do actually impact the world at large in numbers of people and change felt?

What you might look for

You can think of what I'm doing in two dimensions: areas of expertise and capability.

Me

Targeted

Do I want to work for your organization or effort in a unique way?

Experience

Have I spent a significant number of hours in your field? Have I done this before?

Depth

How much expertise do I have in your field? Do I do really hard things in your area?

Passion

Do I care about your field and am I passionate about the work? Do I do more than required?

Gets Things Done

Do I start things myself and take them to finish line? Do I lead and get asked questions?
